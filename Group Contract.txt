Group Contract


If there’s conflicting opinions on what’s the best approach to tackle a problem, our group will do a voting system. The idea with the most votes will be the one we implement. 


Dividing up the tasks early in the project, making sure everyone knows what they have to do. 


Communicate frequently in discord group chat. 


Giving each person a chance to speak whenever making decisions.


Everyone commits their own ideas and shares after. 


Due date for our group will be 2 days prior to the due date set by the professor. 


Everyone must participate. 


When the assignment is complete, we will designate a member to submit the project. 


Tuesdays, Thursdays in the evening are the best time for communication and meeting up.
